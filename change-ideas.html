🌿 Take a Digital Break

📚 Read a Book

Get lost in a story or learn something new—books are the OG screen-free escape.

🚶‍♂️ Go for a Walk

Stretch your legs and let nature refresh your mind—leave your phone behind!

🎨 Be Creative

Paint, draw, doodle, or build—let your imagination have some offline fun.

🧘 Mindfulness & Meditation

Deep breaths. Quiet space. Peaceful vibes. A few minutes of calm does wonders.

💬 IRL Hangouts

Call up your besties, go out for chai, or just vibe offline with real convos.

🏸 Get Moving

Try yoga, dance, sports, or even a quick stretch. Your body will thank you!

🎧 Listen Without Screens

Put on a podcast or music and lie back. No scrolling, just chilling.

✍️ Journal or Brain Dump

Write down your thoughts, dreams, rants—whatever's in your head, let it out.
